## Sector 1: Tourism & Hospitality
*Jamaica receives 4+ million visitors annually. Tourism is 30%+ of GDP.*

### Use Case 1: AI-Powered Hotel Concierge Chatbot
**Role**: Hotel Manager, Front Desk Agent, Guest Relations Officer
**Problem**: Guests ask repetitive questions 24/7; staff overwhelmed at peak times.

**AI Tool**: Claude (claude.ai), ChatGPT, Tidio, Intercom
**Prompt Structure**:
```
You are a friendly concierge at [Hotel Name] in [Location], Jamaica.
You know all about our hotel's amenities, local attractions, restaurant
recommendations, transportation options, and Jamaican culture.
Answer guests' questions warmly and suggest upgrades or activities
when appropriate. Current promotions: [list promotions].
Guest question: [question]
```
**Benefits**: 70% reduction in repetitive staff inquiries, 24/7 coverage, higher guest satisfaction scores
**Implementation**: Deploy on hotel website and WhatsApp Business
