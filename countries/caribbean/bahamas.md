## Sector 1: Tourism and Hospitality

*Tourism accounts for more than 50% of Bahamian GDP. Nassau, Paradise Island, and the Out Islands attract millions of visitors annually, from cruise passengers to ultra-high-net-worth villa guests.*

### Caso de Uso 1: Out Islands Eco-Tourism Package Design

**Rol**: Tourism Operator, Out Island Resort Owner
**Herramienta de IA**: Claude
**Estructura del Prompt**:
```
Design a luxury eco-tourism package for a resort in [island: Exuma/Eleuthera/Abaco/Long Island].
Target guest profile: [ultra-high net worth / adventure traveler / diving enthusiast / family].
Key natural assets: [swimming pigs / blue holes / coral reef / bonefishing / shark diving].
Accommodation type: [eco-villa / boutique resort / private island / liveaboard].
Current nightly rate: [USD amount].
Seasonality: [peak December-April / shoulder May-June / hurricane season July-November].
Nearby attractions within 30 minutes by boat or air taxi: [list].
Sustainability certifications held or sought: [Green Globe / Rainforest Alliance / none].

Create: 3 tiered package offerings (4-night, 7-night, 10-night) with inclusive pricing,
a nature-experience itinerary unique to this island,
SEO-optimized web copy targeting US/UK luxury travel markets,
social media content calendar (Instagram, Facebook — 8 posts),
partnership pitch to Nassau-based dive and air charter operators,
and a sustainability narrative for eco-conscious travelers.
```
**Beneficios**: Reduces package design time by 60%; creates consistent luxury brand voice across all islands

## Sector 2: Financial Services

*The Bahamas is one of the world's top offshore financial centres, home to 250+ banks and trust companies, family offices, and the Bahamas Financial Services Board (BFSB). Nassau manages hundreds of billions in private wealth.*

### Caso de Uso 4: Family Office Investment Policy Statement

**Rol**: Private Banker, Trust Officer, Family Office Advisor
**Herramienta de IA**: Claude
**Estructura del Prompt**:
```
Draft an Investment Policy Statement (IPS) for a family office domiciled in Nassau, Bahamas.
Family profile: [generation number / approximate AUM in USD / family governance structure].
Bahamas legal structure: [Executive Entity / Foundation / International Business Company / Purpose Trust].
Beneficiary profile: [ages, income needs, special needs considerations].
Risk tolerance: [conservative / balanced / growth].
Time horizon: [years].
Asset classes permitted: [equities / fixed income / alternatives / real assets / digital assets].
ESG or impact investing preferences: [yes/no/partial].
Bahamas regulatory context: [SCB / BFSB / Investment Funds Act compliance required].
Reporting currency: [USD — BSD peg removes FX risk].

Create: full IPS document (8-12 pages) with investment objectives,
asset allocation ranges by class and geography,
rebalancing triggers and thresholds,
governance and decision-making matrix,
performance benchmarks appropriate to mandate,
SCB Investment Funds Act compliance checklist,
and annual review schedule.
```
**Beneficios**: Cuts IPS drafting from 3 days to 4 hours while ensuring Bahamian regulatory alignment

## Sector 3: Sand Dollar CBDC and Financial Inclusion

*The Bahamas launched the Sand Dollar — the world's first retail CBDC — in October 2020 via the Central Bank of The Bahamas. Its primary mission is financial inclusion across 700+ islands where traditional banking is inaccessible.*

### Caso de Uso 5: Sand Dollar Merchant Onboarding and Training

**Rol**: Central Bank of The Bahamas Officer, Authorized Financial Institution (AFI), Out Island Merchant
**Herramienta de IA**: Claude
**Estructura del Prompt**:
```
Create a merchant onboarding and financial literacy programme for Sand Dollar CBDC adoption
in an Out Island community in The Bahamas.
Target island/settlement: [Long Island / Cat Island / Andros / Inagua / other].
Current banking infrastructure: [nearest bank branch distance in miles / any ATM present].
Business types in community: [fishing cooperative / small grocery / guest house / craft seller].
Merchant tech literacy level: [basic smartphone user / feature phone only / computer literate].
Wallet platform: [IslandPay / Omni / other AFI].
Connectivity: [reliable LTE / intermittent / satellite only].
Primary barrier to adoption: [trust / tech skills / no smartphone / no formal business registration].
Central Bank support resources available: [workshops / hotline / printed materials].

Produce: a step-by-step merchant onboarding guide in plain English (Grade 6 reading level),
a 30-minute in-person training script for AFI field agents,
FAQ document addressing the 10 most common merchant objections,
troubleshooting guide for offline/poor-connectivity scenarios,
a social media post series for Central Bank awareness campaign (Facebook-first — dominant platform in Family Islands),
and a 6-month adoption tracking template for AFI field officers.
```
**Beneficios**: Accelerates Out Island merchant adoption and reduces financial exclusion across 700+ islands
