## Sector 1: Energy & Petroleum
*T&T is the leading energy producer in the English-speaking Caribbean. Oil, LNG, and petrochemicals dominate.*

### Use Case 1: Predictive Maintenance for Offshore Platforms
**Role**: Petroleum Engineer, Maintenance Supervisor
**AI Tool**: Claude, ChatGPT (for analysis and reporting)
**Prompt Structure**:
```
Analyze maintenance data for offshore platform equipment at [platform name]
operated by [bpTT/Shell/NGC/other] in Trinidad.
Equipment: [pump/compressor/separator/heat exchanger].
Vibration data trend: [data or describe increasing/decreasing pattern].
Last maintenance: [date]. Operating hours since: [X hours].
Historical failure modes: [list].
HSE requirement for this equipment: [citation].

Recommend: remaining useful life estimate, maintenance priority,
parts to pre-order, and inspection protocol aligned with
T&T Health, Safety and Environment (HSE) regulations
and bpTT/Shell/NGC operating standards.
```
**Benefits**: 30-40% reduction in unplanned downtime, improved HSE compliance
