## Sector 1: Yachting and Grenadines Tourism

The Grenadines — comprising Mustique, Bequia, Canouan, Tobago Cays Marine Park, Palm Island, and Union Island — form one of the Caribbean's most celebrated yachting and bareboat charter destinations. The islands attract ultra-high-net-worth visitors, luxury sailing charters, and discerning travelers seeking unspoiled beauty. The Pirates of the Caribbean film series brought global attention to the region's dramatic scenery.

### Use Case 1: Yachting Charter and Grenadines Sailing Itinerary Content Creation

**Role**: Charter Yacht Operator, Grenadines Tourism Marketing Officer, Luxury Travel Agent, SVG Tourism Authority Staff

**AI Tool**: Claude

**Prompt Structure**:

```
You are a luxury yachting and sailing itinerary specialist for the Grenadines, Saint Vincent and the Grenadines.

Create a premium sailing charter package and marketing content using the following information:

- Charter type: [e.g., bareboat / crewed catamaran / superyacht / monohull skippered charter]
- Departure port: [e.g., Blue Lagoon, Saint Vincent / Bequia / Union Island / Rodney Bay, Saint Lucia]
- Charter duration: [e.g., 7 nights / 10 nights / 2 weeks]
- Islands to include: [e.g., Bequia, Mustique, Canouan, Tobago Cays, Palm Island, Mayreau, Union Island]
- Guest profile: [e.g., honeymoon couple, family with teenagers, group of friends, corporate incentive group]
- Budget tier: [e.g., mid-range bareboat / luxury crewed / ultra-premium superyacht]
- Key experiences requested: [e.g., snorkeling Tobago Cays turtle sanctuary, Mustique beach bar, Bequia whale watching history, Pirates of the Caribbean film site, kite surfing at Union Island]
- Provisioning preferences: [e.g., full provisioning / eat ashore / dietary requirements]

Please produce:
1. A day-by-day sailing itinerary with anchorages, highlights, and logistical notes.
2. A premium charter brochure description (400 words) suitable for a luxury travel website.
3. Three social media captions with hashtags for Instagram featuring the Tobago Cays and Mustique.
4. A skipper's briefing document covering key anchorages, local customs, and must-visit spots.
5. A post-charter guest survey to gather feedback and drive repeat bookings.
```

**Benefits**: Drives premium charter bookings, elevates the Grenadines' global reputation among high-net-worth travelers, supports local marine businesses, and extends average visitor spend per trip.

## Sector 2: Arrowroot Production

Saint Vincent and the Grenadines is the world's largest exporter of arrowroot starch, a naturally gluten-free, food-grade starch derived from the Maranta arundinacea plant. SVG arrowroot is used in premium biscuits and baked goods (historically by Huntley and Palmers), pharmaceutical tablet coatings, cosmetics, and increasingly in health food markets. The Saint Vincent Arrowroot Industry Association (SVAIA) oversees production and export.

### Use Case 2: Arrowroot Export Market Development and Premium Branding Strategy

**Role**: SVAIA Marketing Officer, Ministry of Agriculture Trade Specialist, Arrowroot Processing Cooperative Manager

**AI Tool**: Claude

**Prompt Structure**:

```
You are an agricultural export marketing specialist with expertise in niche natural food ingredients and health product markets.

Help me develop an export marketing and branding strategy for Saint Vincent and the Grenadines arrowroot starch using these details:

- Current primary markets: [e.g., UK biscuit manufacturers, pharmaceutical companies, regional Caribbean]
- Target new markets to develop: [e.g., USA health food retail, EU organic food, Asia-Pacific gluten-free market, cosmetics industry]
- Product form: [e.g., bulk food-grade starch / retail consumer packs / pharmaceutical-grade / cosmetic-grade]
- Annual production volume: [e.g., estimated export quantity in metric tons]
- Key selling points to emphasize: [e.g., world's #1 producer, naturally gluten-free, non-GMO, pharmaceutical purity, traceable smallholder farmers, Caribbean origin story]
- Certification status: [e.g., organic certified / Fairtrade / pending / not yet certified]
- Preferred marketing channels: [e.g., trade shows (Natural Products Expo), B2B food ingredient platforms, retail packaging for Whole Foods/Amazon]
- Budget for marketing activities: [Small / Medium — specific budget if known]

Please produce:
1. A premium brand narrative for SVG arrowroot starch targeting international health-food buyers.
2. A product specification sheet suitable for international food ingredient buyers.
3. A trade show pitch script for a Natural Products Expo USA booth presentation.
4. Three B2B email outreach templates for UK, US, and EU food manufacturers.
5. A social media content strategy targeting health-conscious consumer brands on LinkedIn and Instagram.
```

**Benefits**: Increases SVG arrowroot export revenue, diversifies into high-value pharmaceutical and cosmetic markets, supports smallholder farmer incomes, and builds global brand recognition for a uniquely Vincentian product.

## Sector 3: Financial Services and CBI

SVG's financial services sector is regulated by the Financial Services Authority (FSA), and includes International Business Companies (IBCs), an offshore banking sector, insurance, and a Citizenship by Investment program. The CBI program competes in the Eastern Caribbean market alongside Dominica, Saint Lucia, Grenada, and St. Kitts and Nevis.

### Use Case 3: IBC and Offshore Financial Services Client Acquisition Content

**Role**: FSA-Licensed Financial Services Provider, IBC Formation Agent, CBI Program Marketing Officer, Law Firm Partner

**AI Tool**: Claude

**Prompt Structure**:

```
You are a financial services and offshore structuring specialist with expertise in the Eastern Caribbean regulatory environment.

Create client acquisition and educational content for SVG's financial services offerings using these parameters:

- Service to promote: [e.g., IBC formation / offshore bank account / CBI application / trust and estate structuring]
- Target client profile: [e.g., international entrepreneur, e-commerce business owner, family office, high-net-worth individual from emerging market]
- Client's primary objective: [e.g., asset protection / tax efficiency / international business operations / second citizenship / privacy]
- Jurisdiction comparison context: [e.g., SVG vs. BVI vs. Cayman vs. Panama]
- Regulatory framework to highlight: [e.g., SVG FSA oversight, FATF compliance, information exchange treaty status]
- CBI program route if applicable: [e.g., National Transformation Fund / real estate]
- Language and market: [e.g., English-language content for North American market / Spanish for Latin American clients]
- Compliance and due diligence posture: [e.g., emphasize full compliance and transparency / focus on privacy within legal frameworks]

Please produce:
1. A 600-word educational article explaining SVG IBC formation benefits for international entrepreneurs.
2. A comparison table of SVG's CBI program vs. two competing Caribbean programs.
3. Three LinkedIn posts positioning SVG as a legitimate and well-regulated financial centre.
4. An FAQ document answering the 10 most common questions from prospective IBC clients.
5. A professional inquiry response email template for initial client consultations.
```

**Benefits**: Grows the SVG financial services client base, attracts quality international business, strengthens FSA's compliance reputation, and diversifies CBI program revenue beyond regional competition.
