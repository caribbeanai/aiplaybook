## Sector 1: International Business Companies (IBC)

*The BVI is the world's leading IBC jurisdiction. The BVI Business Companies Act governs the formation and administration of BVI Business Companies (BCs). Registered agents, nominee directors, beneficial ownership registers (post-2023 transparency requirements under BOSS — Beneficial Ownership Secure Search system), and substance requirements are central to this sector.*

### Use Case 1: BVI Business Company Formation and Documentation

**Role**: Registered Agent, Corporate Administrator
**AI Tool**: Claude
**Prompt Structure**:
```
Draft a complete formation and onboarding package for a new BVI Business Company.
- Client jurisdiction and residency: [country of UBO/director]
- Structure purpose: [holding company / IP holding / trading / joint venture / SPV]
- Nominee director arrangement: [yes/no — describe if yes]
- Beneficial ownership: [details for BOSS register entry]
- Post-2023 transparency compliance: [BOSS filing obligations, FATCA/CRS status]
- Share structure: [authorized shares, classes, par value or no par value]
- Registered agent: [name of RA firm in Road Town]
- Economic substance test: [relevant activity category under BVI Economic Substance Act]

Create: Memorandum and Articles of Association draft,
first directors resolution,
share certificate template,
BOSS register disclosure checklist,
CRS/FATCA self-certification framework,
economic substance filing calendar,
and client onboarding due diligence checklist per BVI AML Code of Practice.
```
**Benefits**: Reduces drafting time by 60% and ensures post-2023 compliance across BOSS, CRS, and substance requirements in a single workflow.

## Sector 2: Hedge Funds and Investment Vehicles

*The BVI is a leading domicile for hedge funds, private equity, and investment vehicles regulated under SIBA (Securities and Investment Business Act) and administered by the BVI FSC. BVI funds include open-ended and closed-ended structures, professional funds, private funds, and approved funds under BVIFARS (BVI Fund and Regulatory Standards).*

### Use Case 4: BVI Professional Fund Prospectus and Regulatory Filing

**Role**: Fund Administrator, Fund Counsel
**AI Tool**: Claude
**Prompt Structure**:
```
Draft a prospectus and regulatory filing package for a new BVI Professional Fund.
- Fund name: [proposed name]
- Fund structure: [open-ended / closed-ended]
- Investment strategy: [global equity / credit / multi-strategy / crypto / real estate]
- Target investors: [professional investors only — minimum US$100,000 investment]
- Fund manager jurisdiction: [Cayman / BVI / UK / USA / other]
- Administrator: [name of BVI-licensed fund administrator]
- Auditor: [name and jurisdiction]
- SIBA classification sought: [professional fund / private fund / approved fund]
- BVI FSC approval timeline target: [date]
- Redemption terms: [monthly / quarterly / locked-up period]

Create: offering document outline (key sections),
FSC recognition application narrative,
risk factor disclosures for BVI-domiciled funds,
AML policy for investor onboarding,
side pocket provisions language,
and fund governance checklist under SIBA.
```
**Benefits**: Accelerates fund launch timelines and ensures SIBA-compliant documentation from day one, reducing legal review cycles.

## Sector 3: Sailing and Yachting Tourism

*The BVI is one of the world's premier sailing destinations, anchored by the Sir Francis Drake Channel, the Baths at Virgin Gorda, Jost Van Dyke, and Anegada. The charter yacht industry — including bareboat and crewed charters — is a cornerstone of the tourism economy. The BVI Spring Regatta and Sailing Festival is a flagship annual event.*

### Use Case 5: Charter Yacht Marketing and Guest Experience Content

**Role**: Charter Yacht Operator, Tourism Marketing Manager
**AI Tool**: Claude
**Prompt Structure**:
```
Create a comprehensive marketing and guest experience package for a BVI charter yacht operation.
- Vessel name, type, and size: [e.g., 52ft sailing catamaran / motor yacht / classic sloop]
- Charter type offered: [bareboat / crewed / skippered]
- Key BVI sailing destinations featured: [Sir Francis Drake Channel, Virgin Gorda Baths, Jost Van Dyke Soggy Dollar Bar, Anegada, Norman Island, Cooper Island]
- Target guest profile: [experienced sailors / first-time charter guests / honeymooners / corporate group]
- Peak season pricing: [USD per week]
- Differentiator from competitors: [describe]
- Certifications held: [BVI Tourist Board licensed / RYA / ASA]
- Onboard amenities: [describe]

Produce: vessel listing description (300 words, aspirational tone),
7-day BVI itinerary with anchorage highlights,
5 Instagram captions with hashtag sets,
inquiry response email template,
BVI sailing FAQ document for first-time charterers,
provisioning guide for BVI grocery and chandlery,
and post-charter testimonial request email.
```
**Benefits**: Fills charter calendar faster with professional content that converts inquiries to bookings across sailing platforms and social channels.
