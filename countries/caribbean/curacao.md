## Sector 1: Financial Services and Investment Funds

*Curaçao is a long-established offshore financial centre with a sophisticated regulatory framework. CIMA (Centrale Bank van Curaçao en Sint Maarten) oversees banks, investment companies, insurance entities, and trust offices. The Curaçao e-Zone offers tax-advantaged structures for internationally oriented businesses. Wealth management, fund administration, and corporate services are significant employers in Willemstad's financial district.*

### Use Case 1: Curaçao Investment Fund Structuring and CIMA Filing

**Role**: Fund Manager, Legal Counsel, Trust Office Officer
**AI Tool**: Claude
**Prompt Structure**:
```
Prepare a fund structuring and regulatory filing package for a Curaçao investment fund.
- Fund name: [proposed name]
- Fund type: [open-ended investment company / closed-ended fund / private fund / regulated fund under CIMA]
- Investment strategy: [equities / fixed income / real estate / crypto / multi-asset / private equity]
- Investor profile: [professional / institutional / retail — describe minimum investment]
- Fund domicile structure: [NV (Naamloze Vennootschap) / foundation / partnership]
- CIMA licensing category: [Investment Fund / QIIS / exempt fund]
- Administrator: [Curaçao-licensed administrator name]
- Auditor: [Big 4 / local firm and jurisdiction]
- Curaçao e-Zone applicability: [yes / no — describe qualifying activities]
- Target AUM at launch: [USD amount]

Create: fund offering memorandum outline (key sections),
CIMA licence application narrative,
fund governance framework,
AML/CFT investor onboarding policy for Curaçao fund,
e-Zone eligibility assessment,
administrator service agreement key terms,
and regulatory timeline from application to launch.
```
**Benefits**: Compresses fund launch preparation from weeks to days and ensures CIMA-compliant documentation across structuring, licensing, and investor onboarding.

## Sector 2: Tourism

*Curaçao's tourism is anchored by Willemstad's iconic UNESCO-listed Handelskade waterfront, the Hato Caves, pristine Klein Curaçao island, world-class diving at the Curaçao Underwater Park, and the largest Carnival celebration in the Dutch Caribbean. The island draws visitors from the Netherlands, USA, Latin America, and the Caribbean diaspora.*

## Sector 3: E-Gaming Licensing

*Curaçao holds one of the most significant online gambling licensing jurisdictions in the world. The Curaçao Master License (issued under the National Ordinance on Offshore Games of Hazard — NOOGH) allows operators to sub-license and operate online casinos, sports betting, poker, and gaming platforms globally. Regulatory reform efforts are ongoing to modernise the framework through the new Gaming Control Board (GCB).*

### Use Case 4: Curaçao Gaming Licence Application and Compliance

**Role**: Online Gaming Operator, Gaming Licence Consultant
**AI Tool**: Claude
**Prompt Structure**:
```
Prepare a Curaçao e-gaming licence application and compliance package.
- Operator name and jurisdiction of incorporation: [company name / Curaçao NV or foreign entity]
- Gaming products to be offered: [online casino / sports betting / poker / live dealer / lottery]
- Target player markets: [list countries — note restricted jurisdictions: USA, Netherlands, Curaçao itself]
- Master licence holder: [name of Curaçao master licence holder providing sub-licence]
- Platform provider and software: [describe]
- AML/CFT framework for player verification: [KYC process, geofencing, responsible gambling tools]
- Payment processing arrangements: [describe]
- Regulatory reform context: [new Gaming Control Board (GCB) — 2023 reform framework]
- Hosting requirements: [servers in Curaçao / cloud provider]

Create: licence application narrative for GCB/master licence holder,
AML/KYC policy for gaming platform,
terms and conditions framework,
responsible gambling policy,
restricted jurisdiction geofencing list,
payment provider compliance memo,
and regulatory compliance calendar under the new GCB framework.
```
**Benefits**: Accelerates the Curaçao licence application process and ensures the operator has AML, KYC, and responsible gambling documentation ready from day one of operations.
