## Sector 1: Luxury Tourism

Saint Lucia is one of the premier luxury travel destinations in the Caribbean, home to iconic Piton mountains, world-famous Sandals resorts, the annual St. Lucia Jazz and Arts Festival, and a thriving wedding and honeymoon market. The island consistently ranks among the top Caribbean destinations for high-spend visitors.

### Use Case 1: Luxury Resort Guest Experience Personalization and Concierge Content

**Role**: Resort Concierge Manager, Sandals or Anse Chastanet Guest Relations Officer, Tourism Authority Content Strategist

**AI Tool**: Claude

**Prompt Structure**:

```
You are a luxury hospitality specialist with deep expertise in Saint Lucia's high-end tourism market.

Create a personalized guest experience package for an arriving couple using the following details:

- Resort or property: [e.g., Sandals Grande St. Lucian, Jade Mountain, Sugar Beach, Anse Chastanet]
- Guest profile: [e.g., honeymoon couple, anniversary trip, milestone birthday, incentive group]
- Nationality and home country: [e.g., UK, USA, Canada, Germany]
- Length of stay: [e.g., 7 nights]
- Preferred activities: [e.g., Piton hiking, snorkeling at Anse Chastanet, chocolate experience at Rabot Estate, Sulphur Springs mud bath, sailing to the Pitons]
- Dietary preferences or restrictions: [e.g., vegan, gluten-free, no shellfish]
- Budget tier for excursions: [Inclusive / Mid-range extras / Ultra-luxury private charters]
- Special occasion or surprise elements requested: [Yes/No — details if yes]

Please produce:
1. A personalized 7-night welcome letter in warm, luxury brand tone.
2. A curated daily experience recommendation guide with local and resort activities.
3. A private dinner menu suggestion incorporating Saint Lucian cuisine (green fig and saltfish, bouyon, callaloo).
4. Three Instagram caption suggestions for the couple's stay using Saint Lucia's iconic backdrops.
5. A post-stay thank-you note template for the resort to send within 48 hours of departure.
```

**Benefits**: Increases guest satisfaction scores, drives repeat visits and referrals, strengthens Saint Lucia's brand as a world-class luxury destination, and boosts resort review ratings on TripAdvisor and Google.

## Sector 2: Banana and Agriculture

Saint Lucia's banana industry, once the economic backbone of the island, has undergone significant decline since the end of EU preferential trade arrangements. WIBDECO (Windward Islands Banana Development and Exporting Company) continues to operate Fairtrade-certified exports, while the government and farmers are actively diversifying into cocoa, coconut, tropical fruits, and agro-processing.

### Use Case 2: Agricultural Diversification Business Planning for Banana Farmers

**Role**: WIBDECO Agricultural Officer, Saint Lucia Ministry of Agriculture Extension Agent, Farmer Cooperative Leader

**AI Tool**: Claude

**Prompt Structure**:

```
You are an agricultural business advisor specializing in Caribbean small-island farming transitions.

Help me develop a diversification business plan for a banana farmer in Saint Lucia using the following information:

- Farm size and location: [e.g., 5 acres in Dennery / Soufrière / Micoud]
- Current crop: [Fairtrade banana / mixed with plantain / other]
- Proposed diversification crops: [e.g., cocoa, coconut, dasheen, hot pepper, citrus]
- Access to water and irrigation: [Rainfed / Irrigated / Partial]
- Household income dependency on farming: [Primary / Supplemental]
- Market access: [Local market / WIBDECO export / Supermarket / Direct to restaurants]
- Available capital or credit access: [e.g., Development Finance Limited loan / personal savings / grant]
- Farmer's existing skills and equipment: [e.g., tractor access, drying facility, basic processing tools]

Please produce:
1. A 3-year crop transition plan with phased planting calendar.
2. Cost-benefit analysis comparing banana continuation vs. cocoa diversification.
3. A Fairtrade or organic certification pathway summary for the chosen crops.
4. A list of Saint Lucia-specific government grants, NIC support programs, and development finance options.
5. A short farmer profile bio suitable for a Fairtrade marketing campaign.
```

**Benefits**: Supports farmer income resilience, accelerates the shift from monoculture dependency, opens premium export market access, and strengthens rural community economic stability in banana-belt parishes.

## Sector 3: Financial Services and CBI

Saint Lucia's financial services sector includes the Saint Lucia Investment and Immigration Board (SLIIB) overseeing the CBI program, an offshore financial centre, insurance and banking services, and a growing fintech interest. The CBI program competes with Dominica, St. Kitts, Grenada, and Antigua for international investors.

### Use Case 3: CBI Program Marketing Strategy and Agent Recruitment Content

**Role**: SLIIB Marketing Officer, Authorized CBI Agent, Financial Services Promotion Agency Staff

**AI Tool**: Claude

**Prompt Structure**:

```
You are a citizenship-by-investment marketing strategist with expertise in the Eastern Caribbean CBI market.

Develop a targeted marketing strategy for Saint Lucia's CBI program using the following parameters:

- Target investor market: [e.g., high-net-worth individuals from Nigeria, Egypt, Pakistan, China, or the Middle East]
- Investment route to highlight: [e.g., National Economic Fund (NEF) contribution / real estate / enterprise project]
- Competitive differentiator vs. other Caribbean CBI programs: [e.g., lower price point, Piton lifestyle, Nobel Prize prestige, proximity to US]
- Agent network target region: [e.g., UAE, UK, Canada, Singapore]
- Marketing channel: [e.g., international investment conference, digital LinkedIn campaign, agent newsletter, glossy brochure]
- Key lifestyle benefits to emphasize: [e.g., visa-free travel to UK and EU, Saint Lucian passport value, luxury resort lifestyle, family inclusion]
| Processing time and due diligence reputation: [e.g., SLIIB 3-6 month average, five-level due diligence]
- Budget for campaign materials: [e.g., digital-only / print + digital / conference booth package]

Please produce:
1. A 500-word investor-facing program overview for a premium brochure.
2. Three targeted LinkedIn posts for different investor nationalities.
3. An authorized agent recruitment letter inviting migration law firms to partner with SLIIB.
4. A comparison table positioning Saint Lucia's CBI against Dominica, Grenada, and Antigua.
5. A press release announcing a new CBI-funded infrastructure project on the island.
```

**Benefits**: Expands global agent network, increases CBI application volumes, diversifies investor source markets, and elevates Saint Lucia's profile in competitive international investment migration markets.
