## Sector 1: Oil & Gas (Boom Sector)
*ExxonMobil, Hess, and CNOOC operate the Stabroek Block with 11+ billion barrels of recoverable oil.*

### Use Case 1: Local Content Compliance Reporting
**Role**: Local Content Officer, Oil Company Manager
**AI Tool**: Claude, ChatGPT
**Prompt Structure**:
```
Create a Local Content report for [company name] operating in
Guyana's oil and gas sector under the Local Content Act 2021.
Reporting period: [quarter/year].
Guyanese employees: [number, percentage of workforce].
Guyanese vendors contracted: [list with GYD values].
Training provided to Guyanese nationals: [hours, programs].
Local procurement spend: [GYD/USD amount].

Draft: compliant Local Content report for submission to
the Guyana Local Content Secretariat,
gap analysis against Act requirements,
improvement plan for next reporting period,
and narrative on Guyanese workforce development.
```

### Use Case 2: National Resource Revenue Management

Current Natural Resource Fund balance: [USD amount].

### Use Case 3: Oil Spill Response Planning
**Role**: EPA Guyana Officer, Environmental Manager
**Prompt Structure**:
```
Develop an oil spill response plan for offshore Guyana.
Block: Stabroek / [other]. Water depth: [feet].
Nearest sensitive ecosystems: [mangroves/coral/fishing grounds].
Current response capability in Guyana: [describe resources].
Regional support: [Caribbean/international assets].
Regulatory requirement: EPA Guyana / IMO standards.

Create: tiered response framework (Tier 1/2/3),
trajectory modeling considerations for Guyana current patterns,
wildlife protection protocols for Guyana's biodiversity,
community notification plan for fishing communities,
and capacity building recommendations for Guyanese responders.
```

## Sector 2: Agriculture
*Guyana is a major rice and sugar producer.*

### Use Case 4: Rice Precision Agriculture
**Role**: Rice Farmer, NAREI Officer
**Prompt Structure**:
```
Optimize rice production on [X acres] in Guyana's
[Demerara/Essequibo/Berbice] region.
Current yield: [X bags/acre]. Target: [Y bags/acre].
Soil type: [clay/loam]. Water management: [pump/gravity/canal].
Weather: [current season - wet/dry].
Variety: [local variety or hybrid].

Recommend: fertilization plan using products available in Guyana,
pest/disease management (blast, brownspot),
irrigation scheduling,
harvest timing for maximum quality,
post-harvest handling for export quality compliance,
and government NAREI extension support available.
```

## Sector 5: Environment & Biodiversity
*Guyana has 80% forest cover — one of the world's largest pristine tropical forests.*

### Use Case 8: Forest Carbon Credits (REDD+)
**Role**: Ministry of Natural Resources Officer, LCDS Manager
**Prompt Structure**:
```
Develop Guyana's forest carbon credit strategy under the
Low Carbon Development Strategy (LCDS 2030).
Guyana's forest asset: [~18 million hectares, 80% intact].
Current carbon credit sales: [Norway partnership/voluntary carbon market].
Additional market opportunities: [Article 6 Paris Agreement/voluntary market].
Indigenous community involvement: [Amerindian land rights/benefit sharing].

Recommend: carbon credit valuation methodology,
additional buyer markets to approach,
monitoring, reporting and verification (MRV) system improvements,
benefit distribution to Amerindian communities,
and technology solutions for forest monitoring in Guyana.
```
