## Sector 1: Nutmeg and Spices

*Grenada is the world's second largest exporter of nutmeg. The Grenada Cooperative Nutmeg Association (GCNA) manages the supply chain from farmer to export. Key spices include nutmeg, mace, cinnamon, and cloves. AI can help cooperatives, exporters, and smallholder farmers increase value-added processing and market reach.*

### Use Case 1: GCNA Cooperative Export Documentation and Market Intelligence

**Role**: GCNA Export Officer, Spice Cooperative Manager
**AI Tool**: Claude
**Prompt Structure**:
```
You are an international trade and agri-export specialist supporting
the Grenada Cooperative Nutmeg Association (GCNA).

Current crop season: [e.g., main season / off-season]
Current GCNA nutmeg stockpile volume: [metric tonnes]
Current export markets: [e.g., India, EU, USA, Middle East]
Key buyers and pricing: [current contract prices in XCD or USD per lb]
Quality grade breakdown: [Grade A / Grade B / Grade C percentages]
Spice varieties in this export shipment: [nutmeg / mace / cinnamon / cloves]
Target new markets to develop: [e.g., Japan, South Korea, Gulf states]
Compliance requirements for those markets: [e.g., EU MRL pesticide limits, FDA]

Draft:
- A full export documentation checklist for one container shipment (Certificate of Origin, phytosanitary, bill of lading, fumigation)
- A market intelligence brief on the top 3 target new markets — demand trends, price benchmarks, and buyer profiles
- A value-added product pitch deck outline (e.g., ground nutmeg, nutmeg essential oil, nutmeg butter) for EU specialty food buyers
- A farmer price support communication for GCNA members explaining current world nutmeg price and outlook
- A grant proposal outline for GCNA to access CDB (Caribbean Development Bank) agri-processing funding
```
**Benefits**: Reduces export preparation time by 60%, surfaces new market opportunities, and supports farmer communication with clear pricing narratives

## Sector 2: Cocoa and Chocolate

*Grenada produces internationally recognized fine-flavour cocoa. The Grenada Chocolate Company pioneered the bean-to-bar model on the island. Craft chocolate exports to Europe and the USA command premium prices. The Grenada Chocolate Academy (GCNA) trains chocolatiers and supports producers.*

### Use Case 2: Craft Chocolate Export Brand Strategy and Buyer Outreach

**Role**: Chocolate Producer, Grenada Chocolate Academy Coordinator, Cocoa Farmer Cooperative Manager
**AI Tool**: Claude
**Prompt Structure**:
```
You are a specialty food export strategist with expertise in fine-flavour
cocoa and craft chocolate, working with a Grenadian producer.

Producer name / brand: [e.g., Grenada Chocolate Company / artisan brand name]
Farm location and terroir notes: [e.g., Hermitage Estate, volcanic soil, elevation]
Cocoa variety: [Trinitario / Forastero / Criollo blend — describe flavour profile]
Current certifications: [Organic / Fairtrade / Rainforest Alliance / none]
Chocolate product range: [bar sizes, percentages, inclusions — e.g., 71% dark, spiced with Grenadian nutmeg]
Current export markets: [e.g., UK, USA, Canada — wholesale or direct-to-consumer]
Target new buyers: [specialty food retailers / chocolate subscription boxes / Michelin-starred restaurants]
Price point: [USD per bar retail / per kg couverture wholesale]
Chocolate Academy partnership: [training programmes offered, certifications available]

Create:
- A brand story narrative (300 words) emphasising Grenada's volcanic terroir, bean-to-bar heritage, and fine-flavour cocoa credentials — suitable for export buyer presentation
- A specialty buyer cold outreach email (150 words) targeting high-end UK or US chocolate retailers
- A flavour wheel description and tasting notes for the flagship product
- A Grenadian Chocolate Academy programme outline to attract international chocolatier students (market: UK, USA, Japan culinary tourism)
- A sustainability and traceability narrative aligned with EU Deforestation Regulation (EUDR) requirements
- A pricing strategy analysis: retail, wholesale, and direct online channels
```
**Benefits**: Strengthens premium brand positioning, opens export buyer relationships, and supports chocolatier tourism as an ancillary revenue stream

## Sector 3: Tourism

*Tourism is one of Grenada's fastest-growing sectors. Key attractions include Grand Anse Beach (consistently ranked among the Caribbean's finest), the Underwater Sculpture Park (world's first underwater sculpture museum), spice estate tours, and the Citizenship by Investment (CBI) program, which drives real estate development and luxury tourism infrastructure.*

### Use Case 3: Spice Isle Tourism Campaign and CBI Lifestyle Marketing

**Role**: Grenada Tourism Authority Marketing Manager, CBI Program Officer, Boutique Hotel Owner
**AI Tool**: Claude
**Prompt Structure**:
```
You are a Caribbean destination marketing specialist and investment promotion
advisor for Grenada's tourism and Citizenship by Investment (CBI) program.

Target visitor segments: [luxury travellers / eco-tourists / culinary tourists / diving enthusiasts]
Key tourism assets to feature: [Grand Anse Beach / Underwater Sculpture Park / spice estate tours / chocolate tours / Levera Beach / waterfalls]
Hotel / accommodation inventory: [luxury resorts, boutique inns, CBI-funded villa developments — list names]
CBI program details: [investment threshold in USD, real estate options approved, passport strength — Grenadian passport visa-free access countries]
Target CBI investor nationalities: [e.g., Chinese, Middle Eastern, Russian, US citizens via E-2 treaty]
Current tourism arrivals data: [stayover arrivals last year, cruise arrivals, top source markets]
High season: [December – April]
Key festivals: [Grenada Sailing Festival, Spicemas Carnival — August]
Direct airlift: [airlines serving Maurice Bishop International Airport]

Create:
- A 12-month tourism marketing calendar integrating Grand Anse beach season, Spicemas Carnival (August), Grenada Sailing Festival, and culinary / spice tourism peaks
- A "Spice Isle Experience" 7-day itinerary combining beach, diving the Underwater Sculpture Park, nutmeg estate tour, chocolate academy visit, and rum distillery
- A CBI real estate investor pitch document (2 pages) highlighting Grenada's E-2 treaty with the USA, passport strength, lifestyle, and ROI on CBI-approved real estate
- Five Instagram / Facebook captions for each key attraction, optimised for international luxury travel audiences
- A press release for travel media announcing a new Grenada culinary tourism initiative
- A crisis communication template for hurricane season reassurance messaging to prospective visitors and CBI investors
```
**Benefits**: Coordinates marketing across tourism and investment promotion, drives high-value visitor and CBI investor interest, and maximises seasonal revenue
