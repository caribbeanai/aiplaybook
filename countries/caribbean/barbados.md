## Sector 1: International Business & Finance
*Barbados is a premier offshore financial centre with treaty networks and IBC structures.*

### Use Case 1: International Business Company (IBC) Administration
**Role**: Corporate Services Provider, Financial Consultant
**AI Tool**: Claude, ChatGPT
**Prompt Structure**:
```
Create documentation for an International Business Company (IBC)
incorporated in Barbados.
Structure purpose: [holding company/IP holding/trading/investment].
Client jurisdiction: [client's home country].
Treaty benefits sought: [Barbados tax treaty with Canada/US/UK/other].
Activities: [description of business activities].

Create: corporate resolution templates,
annual maintenance checklist,
treaty benefits eligibility analysis,
BEPS (Base Erosion and Profit Shifting) compliance review,
CRS/FATCA reporting obligations,
and fee schedule for ongoing maintenance.
```
**Benefits**: 50% time reduction in documentation preparation
