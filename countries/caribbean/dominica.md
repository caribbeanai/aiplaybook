## Sector 1: Citizenship by Investment (CBI)

Dominica operates one of the oldest and most reputable CBI programs in the world, administered by the Citizenship by Investment Unit (CBIU). The program attracts high-net-worth individuals globally and is a major contributor to government revenue.

### Use Case 1: CBI Application Pre-Screening and Eligibility Assessment

**Role**: CBI Processing Officer, Authorized Marketing Agent, Immigration Consultant

**AI Tool**: Claude

**Prompt Structure**:

```
You are an expert advisor on Dominica's Citizenship by Investment (CBI) program.

Help me pre-screen a prospective applicant using the following details:

- Applicant nationality: [e.g., Nigerian, Lebanese, Russian]
- Investment option being considered: [Real Estate / National Development Fund (NDF) contribution]
- Minimum investment threshold awareness: [Yes/No — applicant understands $100,000 NDF or $200,000 real estate minimum]
- Number of dependents to be included: [e.g., spouse + 2 children]
- Any prior visa refusals or criminal record declared: [Yes/No/Details]
- Source of funds documentation available: [e.g., business sale proceeds, inheritance, salary history]
- Preferred authorized agent or law firm: [Name of firm if known]
- Timeline for desired citizenship: [e.g., within 6 months, flexible]

Based on this profile:
1. Assess basic eligibility under CBIU guidelines.
2. Identify any red flags that could delay or disqualify the application.
3. Recommend the most suitable investment route with cost breakdown.
4. List the key due diligence documents the applicant should prepare.
5. Draft a professional introductory summary letter for the agent to use with the CBIU.
```

**Benefits**: Reduces application errors, speeds up pre-screening, improves agent-client communication, and strengthens Dominica's reputation for a rigorous and efficient CBI process.

## Sector 2: Eco-Tourism

Dominica — known as the "Nature Isle of the Caribbean" — offers world-class whale watching, scuba diving, rainforest hiking, the famous Boiling Lake, and the UNESCO-designated Morne Trois Pitons National Park. Tourism infrastructure is growing following post-hurricane investment.

### Use Case 2: Personalized Eco-Tour Itinerary Builder for Nature Visitors

**Role**: Tour Operator, Hotel Concierge, Discover Dominica Authority Marketing Officer

**AI Tool**: Claude

**Prompt Structure**:

```
You are an expert eco-tourism planner for Dominica, the Nature Isle of the Caribbean.

Design a personalized multi-day itinerary for a visitor using these details:

- Trip duration: [e.g., 5 days / 7 days]
- Visitor fitness level: [Low / Moderate / High — relevant for Boiling Lake hike]
- Primary interests: [e.g., whale watching, diving at Champagne Reef, Kalinago Territory, hiking, birdwatching]
- Accommodation base: [e.g., Roseau, Calibishie, Soufrière, eco-lodge in the interior]
- Travel party: [Solo / Couple / Family with children / Group]
- Budget range per day (XCD or USD): [e.g., budget, mid-range, luxury]
- Any accessibility or dietary considerations: [Details]
- Preferred pace: [Relaxed / Active / Packed schedule]

Please provide:
1. A day-by-day itinerary with morning, afternoon, and evening activities.
2. Recommended local guides and operators for each activity.
3. Best times of year to visit for whale watching (sperm whales are resident year-round).
4. A packing list specific to Dominica's rainforest terrain and weather.
5. A short promotional description of the itinerary suitable for social media or a travel blog.
```

**Benefits**: Enhances visitor experience, drives longer stays and higher spend, promotes indigenous Kalinago culture responsibly, and supports local guides and operators.

## Sector 3: Geothermal Energy

Dominica is pursuing energy independence through the Wotten Waven geothermal project, managed in partnership with DOMLEC (Dominica Electricity Services). The island sits on significant volcanic geothermal resources and aims to become a net exporter of clean energy to neighboring islands.

### Use Case 3: Geothermal Project Community Communication and Stakeholder Briefing

**Role**: DOMLEC Communications Officer, Government Energy Policy Advisor, Project Manager

**AI Tool**: Claude

**Prompt Structure**:

```
You are a specialist in energy communications and community engagement for small island developing states (SIDS).

Help me prepare a stakeholder communication package for Dominica's Wotten Waven geothermal energy project using this context:

- Target audience: [e.g., Wotten Waven village residents / national media / regional investors / OECS energy ministers]
- Current project phase: [e.g., exploratory drilling / construction / commissioning]
- Key concerns from community: [e.g., noise, land use, environmental impact, employment promises]
- Projected energy capacity: [e.g., 7 MW initial phase, expandable to 30 MW]
- Partners involved: [e.g., DOMLEC, Government of Dominica, international development partners]
- Benefits to highlight: [e.g., reduced electricity bills, energy independence, export revenue to Guadeloupe/Martinique]
- Language preference for community materials: [English / Antillean Creole Patois / both]
- Specific message to convey: [e.g., project is on schedule / new jobs available / environmental assessment results]

Produce:
1. A plain-language community FAQ document (max 1 page).
2. A formal press release for national and regional media.
3. Talking points for a town hall meeting with Wotten Waven residents.
4. A one-page investor briefing summary.
5. Suggested social media posts for the Government of Dominica's official channels.
```

**Benefits**: Builds community trust, reduces misinformation, accelerates project social license, and positions Dominica as a model for clean energy transition in the Eastern Caribbean.
