## Sector 1: Tourism and Eco-Tourism
*Belize is one of the Caribbean's most distinctive tourism destinations — the Belize Barrier Reef System is the second largest coral reef in the world and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The country offers a rare combination of marine, jungle, and archaeological experiences including the Great Blue Hole, jaguar reserves, cave tubing, and iconic Maya ruins.*

### Use Case 1: Multi-Experience Tourism Package Design
**Role**: Tour Operator, Belize Tourism Board (BTB) Officer, Lodge Owner, Maya Site Concession Manager
**AI Tool**: Claude
**Prompt Structure**:
```
Design a premium multi-experience tourism package for
[tour operator / lodge / travel agency] in Belize targeting
[US / European / Canadian / adventure travellers].

Duration: [number of days / nights].
Primary experiences: [barrier reef diving / Blue Hole / jungle lodge / Maya ruins / cave tubing / jaguar reserve].
Accommodation: [San Pedro / Hopkins / Placencia / inland lodge — name and category].
Visitor profile: [budget / mid-range / luxury / family / couple / adventure].
Budget per person: [USD range].
Season: [winter peak / shoulder / summer].
Special interests: [birding / diving certification / archaeology / culture / fishing].
Current booking volume: [number of guests per month / year].
Distribution channels in use: [website / OTAs / travel agents / direct].

Create:
- 3 differentiated itinerary packages (short/medium/extended stay),
- pricing strategy in USD benchmarked against comparable Caribbean and Central American destinations,
- description of Belize Barrier Reef UNESCO and Blue Hole experience for marketing materials,
- content for Belize Tourism Board (BTB) compliant marketing collateral,
- digital marketing strategy (Instagram, YouTube, TripAdvisor, dive travel platforms),
- partnership recommendations (Caribbean Airlines, Tropic Air, Belize Dive operators),
- sustainable tourism practices aligned with BTB eco-certification standards,
- and upsell strategy (private charters, specialist guides, exclusive experiences).
```
**Benefits**: Creates higher-value tourism packages, increases average spend per visitor, differentiates Belize from mass-market Caribbean destinations, and supports BTB's strategy of attracting quality-over-quantity tourism.

## Sector 2: Agriculture — Sugar, Citrus, and Banana Export
*Agriculture remains a cornerstone of the Belizean economy. The sugar industry centred in the Corozal and Orange Walk districts is anchored by Belize Sugar Industries (BSI/ASR), the citrus industry is concentrated in Stann Creek District, and banana production is primarily exported through WIBDECO to the European market under preferential trade arrangements.*

### Use Case 2: Sugar Cane Smallholder Productivity and Compliance
**Role**: BSI/ASR Outgrower Manager, Smallholder Farmer, Belize Sugar Cane Farmers Association (BSCFA) Officer, Ministry of Agriculture Extension Officer
**AI Tool**: Claude
**Prompt Structure**:
```
Optimise sugar cane productivity and export compliance for
smallholder farmers supplying [BSI / ASR Sugar] in Belize.

District: [Corozal / Orange Walk].
Farm size: [acres].
Current yield: [tonnes of cane per acre].
Target yield: [tonnes per acre].
Soil type: [description].
Water availability: [rainfed / irrigated].
Current fertiliser programme: [describe or "none"].
Pest and disease challenges: [ratoon stunting disease / rust / others].
Export market: [UK / EU under CARIFORUM-EU EPA].
BSI factory delivery schedule: [harvest season start/end].
Farmer association: [BSCFA / other].

Develop:
- precision agronomy plan for Belize's Orange Walk / Corozal agro-climatic conditions,
- fertilisation schedule using products available in Belize,
- integrated pest and disease management programme,
- ratoon crop management strategy to extend field life,
- irrigation water use efficiency recommendations,
- EU food safety and sustainability compliance checklist (Bonsucro / Rainforest Alliance),
- BSI delivery scheduling to maximise sucrose content at harvest,
- income projection under current and improved yields,
- and government extension support available through Belize Ministry of Agriculture.
```
**Benefits**: Increases smallholder income, improves Belize's sugar export competitiveness under EU preferential access, reduces input costs, and strengthens the long-term viability of the BSI supply chain.

## Sector 3: Financial Services — Offshore and International Business
*Belize is a well-established offshore financial services jurisdiction, offering International Business Companies (IBCs), offshore banking, trusts, and foundations. The International Financial Services Commission (IFSC) regulates the sector. Belize's English common law system, USD-pegged currency, and political stability make it an attractive jurisdiction for international structures, though the country has faced ongoing pressure to comply with FATF and OECD global standards.*

### Use Case 3: International Business Company (IBC) Structuring and Compliance
**Role**: IFSC-Licensed Financial Services Provider, Corporate Service Provider, International Client Advisor, Legal Counsel
**AI Tool**: Claude
**Prompt Structure**:
```
Draft a compliance and client advisory brief for
[corporate services firm / law firm / financial services provider]
registered with the IFSC Belize regarding International Business Companies (IBCs).

Client profile: [individual / family office / trading company / holding structure].
Client nationality: [country].
Purpose of IBC: [international trading / holding company / asset protection / invoice processing].
Current FATF/OECD context: [describe Belize's current compliance status or greylist concerns].
Substance requirements: [OECD BEPS / economic substance rules applicable].
Reporting obligations: [CRS / FATCA / beneficial ownership registry].
Banking: [Belize bank / offshore correspondent / international bank].
Jurisdiction alternatives considered: [BVI / Cayman / Nevis / Seychelles].

Produce:
- IBC structure overview and legitimate use cases under Belize IBCs Act (Cap. 270),
- compliance checklist (IFSC, beneficial ownership, CRS/FATCA, economic substance),
- comparison of Belize IBC vs. BVI / Cayman / Nevis for the stated purpose,
- risk assessment and mitigation for operating in a jurisdiction under OECD/FATF review,
- recommended banking solutions for Belize IBC (correspondent banking access),
- client due diligence (CDD) and KYC documentation list,
- ongoing annual compliance calendar,
- and guidance on legitimate substance and operational requirements to maintain structure integrity.
```
**Benefits**: Helps Belizean financial services providers deliver compliant, well-structured solutions that meet international standards, protect client interests, and maintain Belize's competitive position as a credible IBC jurisdiction.
