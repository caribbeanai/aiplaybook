## Regional Logistics Context

- Panama Canal handles 5% of global trade — AI can optimize operations
- Kingston, Jamaica is a major Caribbean transshipment hub
- Brazil has LATAM's most complex logistics (continental scale, poor infrastructure)
- Last-mile delivery is a major challenge across the region
- E-commerce growth driving logistics tech adoption

## 30+ Logistics AI Use Cases

### Port & Shipping
```
Prompt: "Optimize vessel operations at [port name] in [country]:
Current vessel queue: [list vessels, ETAs, cargo types].
Berth availability: [X berths, availability schedule].
Equipment status: [cranes, yard tractors - operational/maintenance].
Labour shift schedule: [gangs available per shift].
Weather forecast: [upcoming conditions].
Cargo priority: [list time-sensitive cargo].
Optimize: berth allocation, equipment deployment, labour planning,
and forecast throughput for next 48 hours."
```

### Last-Mile Delivery (Latin America)
```
Prompt: "Optimize last-mile delivery for [e-commerce company/courier]
in [Latin American city: São Paulo/Mexico City/Bogotá/Lima/Santiago].
Daily deliveries: [X packages]. Service area: [districts/neighborhoods].
Fleet: [X motorcycles, Y vans, Z cargo bikes].
Delivery time windows: [X hours, specific customer windows: list].
Traffic data: [peak hours in city, restricted zones].
Failed delivery rate: [X]%.
Create: daily route optimization, alternative delivery methods for
[high-density informal settlements/favelas/comunas],
rescheduling workflow for failures, and KPI dashboard."
```

### Customs Clearance Automation
```
Prompt: "Streamline customs clearance for a [importer/exporter/broker]
operating through [port/airport] in [country].
Common shipment types: [description]. Volume: [X shipments/month].
Current clearance time: [X days]. Target: [Y days].
Common delays: [list].
Customs authority: [ASYCUDA system/local customs name].
Create: documentation checklist by product type,
pre-clearance preparation workflow,
common error prevention guide,
customs broker briefing template,
and risk assessment for consignments."
```

### Cold Chain Management (Agriculture Exports)
```
Prompt: "Design a cold chain management system for [product: avocado/flowers/fish/pharma]
export from [country] to [destination].
Product temperature requirement: [range].
Export volume: [X tonnes/week].
Route: [farm → packhouse → port/airport → destination].
Pain points: [temperature excursions/delays/documentation].
Recommend: temperature monitoring technology (IoT sensors),
alert protocols, documentation for HACCP compliance,
contingency procedures for cold chain breaks,
and technology options available in [country] at [budget level]."
```
