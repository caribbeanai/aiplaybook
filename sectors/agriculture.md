## Universal Agriculture AI Use Cases

### 1. Crop Disease Diagnosis
**AI Tool**: Claude, Plantix (free mobile app), ChatGPT
**Prompt**:
```
I am a farmer in [country], [region/department/parish].
I grow [crop] on [X] hectares/acres.
My plants are showing these symptoms: [describe: yellowing leaves/
spots/wilting/stunted growth/unusual coloration].
Affected percentage: [X]%.
Recent weather: [dry/wet/temperature extremes].
Soil type: [clay/sandy/loamy].

Diagnose the most likely disease or pest, recommend treatment
using products available in [country], provide prevention strategy,
and estimate yield impact if untreated vs. treated.
```

### 2. Precision Fertilization Plan
**AI Tool**: Claude, ChatGPT
**Prompt**:
```
Create a fertilization plan for [crop] in [country].
Soil test results: pH [X], N [X], P [X], K [X], Organic Matter [X]%.
Crop growth stage: [stage].
Target yield: [X tonnes/hectares].
Available fertilizers in my area: [list].
Budget: [amount in local currency].
Water availability: [irrigated/rain-fed].
Recommend: fertilizer type, application rates, timing schedule,
expected yield improvement, and cost-benefit analysis.
```

### 3. Climate-Smart Farming Calendar
**Countries**: All | **AI Tool**: Claude, ChatGPT
**Prompt**:
```
Create a 12-month farming calendar for [crop type] in [country], [region].
Climate type: [tropical/subtropical/highland/coastal].
Rainfall pattern: [wet season months / dry season months].
Common climate risks: [hurricanes/drought/flooding/frost].
Land size: [X hectares/acres].
Market target: [local/export/processing].
Include: planting dates, irrigation schedule, fertilization calendar,
pest/disease monitoring timeline, harvest schedule,
post-harvest activities, and climate risk mitigation actions.
```

### 4. Agricultural Export Documentation
**AI Tool**: Claude, ChatGPT
**Prompt**:
```
I want to export [product] from [country] to [destination market: US/EU/UK/Japan].
My product: [description, organic/conventional, volume].
Help me: identify phytosanitary requirements,
packaging and labeling specifications for [market],
required certifications (GlobalGAP, organic, fair trade),
documentation checklist for customs,
common compliance failures and how to avoid them,
and national export support programs available in [country].
```

### 5. Farm Business Plan
**AI Tool**: Claude, ChatGPT
**Prompt**:
```
Create a [1-year/3-year/5-year] business plan for a [crop/livestock] farm in [country].
Farm size: [X hectares/acres] in [region/department].
Current production: [description or "starting from scratch"].
Available investment: [amount in local currency].
Market target: [local market/export/agro-processing].
Water resources: [description].
Create: production plan, investment schedule, revenue projections,
break-even analysis, risk assessment with mitigation,
available government subsidies and programs in [country],
and market access strategy.
```
