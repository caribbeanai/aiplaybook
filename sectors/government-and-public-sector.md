## 40+ Government AI Use Cases

### Policy Development
```
Prompt: "Draft a [policy brief/white paper/cabinet note] for the
Government of [country] on [policy topic].
Problem: [describe the public policy problem].
Current legislation/policy: [existing framework].
Stakeholders affected: [list].
Evidence base: [available data].
Proposed options: [list].
Target audience: [Ministers/Parliament/public].
Structure: Executive Summary, Background, Analysis, Options,
Recommendation, Implementation Plan, Resource Requirements.
Use [country]-specific data and CARICOM/regional context where relevant."
```

### Citizen Services Automation
```
Prompt: "Design an AI-powered citizen service chatbot for the
[Ministry/Agency] of [country].
Services to automate: [list].
Most common queries: [list top 10 citizen questions].
Technology constraints: [mobile-first, [X]% smartphone penetration,
internet quality in [country]].
Languages required: [list - include local creoles if relevant].
Create: conversation flows for top 5 services,
FAQ database (50 Q&As), escalation protocol,
accessibility features, and implementation roadmap."
```

### Parliamentary Research
```
Prompt: "Research the following for a [Member of Parliament/Senator]
in [country]'s Parliament:
Topic: [policy/bill/national issue].
Position to support: [description].
Time: available [X hours] for research.
Provide: factual background, relevant statistics for [country],
regional comparisons (CARICOM/LatAm), legal/constitutional considerations,
5 strongest arguments, anticipated counter-arguments,
and source recommendations for further research."
```

### Procurement & Contract Management
```
Prompt: "Create procurement documentation for [government entity] in [country]:
Project: [description]. Budget: [amount in local currency].
Timeline: [start/end dates].
Generate: Terms of Reference (ToR), evaluation criteria matrix,
compliance checklist against [country's procurement law],
supplier communication template,
and contract monitoring framework.
Ensure compliance with [national procurement regulations]."
```

### Regulatory Impact Assessment
```
Prompt: "Conduct a Regulatory Impact Assessment for a proposed
[regulation/law] in [country]:
Proposed regulation: [description].
Problem being addressed: [market failure/public safety/etc.].
Affected sectors: [list].
Estimated affected businesses: [X SMEs, Y large businesses].
Enforcement cost: [estimated].

Assess: economic impacts (positive/negative),
social impacts, environmental implications,
administrative burden on businesses,
MSME-specific impacts,
alternatives to regulation,
and international/CARICOM compatibility."
```
