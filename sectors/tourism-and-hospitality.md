## The Opportunity

Tourism is the **backbone of the Caribbean economy** and a **major driver** across Latin America:
- Caribbean tourism: $37 billion+ annually, 15-80% of GDP by country
- Latin America attracts 100M+ international arrivals annually
- 1 in 5 Caribbean jobs is in tourism
- AI can increase tourism revenue by 15-30% while improving visitor satisfaction

## 50+ Tourism AI Use Cases

### Revenue Management
```
## Use Case: Dynamic Pricing Strategy
Prompt Template:
"Analyze my [hotel/tour/airline] pricing for [destination]:
Current rates: [list]. Competitor rates: [list].
Occupancy trend: [X]%. Upcoming events: [list].
Booking pace vs last year: [comparison].
Seasonal patterns: [peaks/troughs].
Recommend: optimal pricing for next 90 days by room type/product,
promotional packages for soft periods, and revenue projection."
```

### Tour Operations
```
## Use Case: Custom Itinerary Generator
Prompt Template:
"Create a custom [X]-day itinerary for [destination: Jamaica/Colombia/Peru/etc.].
Traveler type: [family with kids 8-12/couple honeymoon/solo female/group of friends].
Travel style: [adventure/cultural/relaxation/luxury/budget].
Starting point: [city/airport].
Interests: [list].
Dietary needs: [list].
Budget per person: [USD range].
Must-see: [non-negotiables].
Avoid: [list].

Include: daily schedule with times, transport between activities,
restaurant recommendations (local, not just tourist traps),
estimated costs, booking tips, cultural etiquette notes,
safety advice, and packing suggestions for this specific destination."
```

### Booking Optimization
```
## Use Case: OTA Listing Optimization
Prompt Template:
"Optimize my [hotel/guesthouse/tour] listing on [Booking.com/Airbnb/Expedia/Viator].
Property name: [name]. Location: [destination].
Current listing: [paste description].
Unique selling points: [list what makes you special].
Target guest: [description].
Competitors getting better reviews cite: [research findings].

Rewrite: title, description (300 words), neighborhood section,
house rules (friendly tone), and host bio.

and suggest 5 additional amenities to mention."
```

### Crisis Communication
