## Regional Finance Context

- Caribbean offshore financial centres manage trillions in assets (Cayman, BVI, Barbados)
- Latin America has some of the world's most innovative fintech (Nubank, Mercado Pago, Rappi)
- 40%+ of Latin Americans remain unbanked — huge opportunity for AI-powered inclusion
- Remittances to Latin America & Caribbean: $150+ billion/year — AI can reduce costs
- High mobile penetration enables mobile-first financial services

## 50+ Finance AI Use Cases

### Credit Underwriting & Risk
```
Prompt: "Analyze this loan application for a [business type] in [country]:
Business name: [name]. Years in operation: [X].
Annual revenue: [amount in local currency].
Monthly cash flow (last 6 months): [figures].
Existing debt obligations: [list].
Assets/collateral: [description].
Credit history: [description or score if available].
Purpose of loan: [description].
Loan amount requested: [amount].

Assess creditworthiness, calculate key ratios (Debt Service Coverage,
Debt-to-Income), identify risk factors, suggest appropriate loan terms,
and flag any concerns requiring further investigation.
Comply with [country's banking regulations]."
```

### AML/KYC Compliance
```
Prompt: "Review the following customer profile and transaction history
for AML compliance under [country's] regulations and FATF guidelines:
Customer type: [individual/corporate].
Customer profile: [description - jurisdiction, industry, PEP status].
Transaction pattern: [describe: amounts, frequency, counterparties, geographies].
Red flags identified by system: [list].

Conduct risk assessment, identify specific AML typologies present,
recommend: enhanced due diligence steps, SAR filing decision,
transaction monitoring parameters, and regulatory reporting obligations
under [country's Financial Intelligence Unit requirements]."
```

### Financial Advisory
```
Prompt: "Create a personalized financial plan for a client in [country]:
Age: [X]. Income: [amount in local currency]. Family situation: [description].
Assets: [list]. Debts: [list]. Risk tolerance: [conservative/moderate/aggressive].
Financial goals: [short/medium/long-term goals].
Timeline to retirement: [X years].

Recommend: savings allocation, investment options available in [country]
(local stock exchange, pension funds, government bonds, mutual funds),
tax-efficient strategies under [country] law,
insurance needs, and emergency fund target.
Explain in language suitable for a [financial literacy level] client."
```

### Remittance Optimization
```
Prompt: "Help a [country] family optimize their remittance strategy:
Sender location: [city, country].
Recipient location: [city, country].
Amount sent monthly: [USD/local currency].
Current service used: [Western Union/MoneyGram/other].
Current fees paid: [X]%.
Recipient's banking access: [banked/unbanked/mobile money].

Compare all major remittance corridors between [origin] and [destination],
identify lowest-cost options meeting recipient's access needs,
calculate annual savings from switching,
and explain digital alternatives available in both countries
(Wise, Remitly, mobile money platforms)."
```

### Insurance Products
```
Prompt: "Design an insurance product tailored for [target market] in [country]:
Target segment: [microentrepreneurs/smallholders/gig workers/SMEs].
Key risks faced: [hurricane/crop failure/theft/health/income loss].
Premium affordability: [max monthly amount in local currency].
Distribution channel: [mobile/agent/bank branch/cooperative].
Regulatory framework: [country's insurance regulator].

Design: coverage scope, premium structure, claims process
(simple enough for target market), distribution partnership model,
and technology platform requirements for [country's connectivity]."
```

### Fintech Product Development
```
Prompt: "I am building a fintech product in [country] for [target segment]:
Problem: [specific financial problem being solved].
Target users: [demographics, current behavior].
Regulatory environment: [central bank fintech sandbox/existing regulations].
Competing products: [list].
Technology: [mobile app/USSD/web/WhatsApp].

Design: product features (MVP), user flow,
revenue model, compliance roadmap,
partnership requirements (banking license partner, etc.),
and go-to-market strategy for [country].
Reference: [relevant regulatory frameworks and any active fintech sandbox programs]."
```
