## Regional Energy Context

- Caribbean imports 90%+ of energy — highest energy costs in the world
- Latin America is energy rich: Venezuela (oil), Brazil (oil+hydro+biofuels), Trinidad (LNG), Guyana (new oil), Chile (solar+wind+lithium), Colombia (coal+oil)
- Energy transition opportunity: Caribbean solar/wind, Chile Atacama solar, Uruguay renewables
- AI can reduce energy costs by 20-30% in Caribbean businesses

## 40+ Energy AI Use Cases

### Renewable Energy Planning
```
Prompt: "Assess renewable energy potential for [country/location]:
Current energy mix: [fossil fuel X%, renewable Y%].
Renewable resources: [solar irradiance data if available, wind resources, geothermal for Caribbean].
Peak demand: [X MW]. Population: [Y].
Grid constraints: [describe current grid issues].
Government targets: [country's renewable energy targets].
Recommend: optimal renewable mix for [country],
technology priorities (solar/wind/geothermal/hydro),
storage requirements, grid modernization needs,
investment required, and regional energy trade opportunities."
```

### Oil & Gas Operations (T&T, Guyana, Venezuela, Colombia, Brazil)
```
Prompt: "Analyze this oil & gas operational data for [company] in [country]:
Production data: [barrels/day, decline rate, operating costs].
Equipment performance: [key equipment status].
Safety incidents (last 12 months): [list].
Regulatory compliance: [HSE/Ministry status].
Optimization goal: [reduce opex/increase production/improve safety].
Identify: top 3 operational improvements,
predictive maintenance priorities,
cost reduction opportunities,
and regulatory risk mitigation actions."
```

### Energy Efficiency for Caribbean SMEs
```
Prompt: "Conduct an energy efficiency analysis for a [business type]
in [Caribbean country] with monthly electricity bill of [amount].
Current equipment: [list major energy-consuming equipment].
Operating hours: [X hours/day, Y days/week].
Utility provider: [local utility, e.g., JPS Jamaica, T&TEC, BL&P Barbados].
Current energy rate: [rate per kWh].
Investment budget: [amount].
Identify: highest-impact efficiency measures,
ROI calculation for each, quick wins vs. capital projects,
solar feasibility analysis at current utility rates,
and available government incentives in [country]."
```

### Caribbean Energy Transition
```
Prompt: "Develop a national energy transition roadmap for [Caribbean island]:
Current situation: [all-diesel / partial renewables, energy cost per kWh].
Population: [X]. Peak demand: [X MW].
Renewable resources: [solar/wind/geothermal assessment].
Grid characteristics: [island grid, limited interconnection].
Financial constraints: [government budget limitations].
International climate commitments: [CARICOM energy targets, NDC].
Create: 10-year transition roadmap,
investment priorities,
financing mechanisms (IDB, CDB, Green Climate Fund),
local employment opportunities in clean energy,
and consumer tariff impact analysis."
```
