## The Creative Economy

Our region has extraordinary creative assets:
- Jamaica: reggae, dancehall — global cultural exports
- Trinidad & Tobago: Carnival, soca, calypso, steelpan
- Brazil: samba, bossa nova, funk, Carnival; growing global film presence
- Colombia: salsa (Cali), vallenato; film renaissance
- Mexico: mariachi, corridos, telenovelas (global); vibrant art scene
- Argentina: tango; strong film, literature, fashion
- Cuba: son, rumba, salsa origins; world-class art

## 30+ Creative Industries AI Use Cases

### Music Industry
```
Prompt: "Create a digital marketing plan for a [reggae/dancehall/soca/
salsa/samba/cumbia/vallenato/mariachi/tango] artist releasing new music.
Artist: [name/description]. New release: [song/EP/album title].
Current following: [platforms, follower counts].
Target markets: [Caribbean/LatAm/US diaspora/global].
Budget: [amount USD].
Create: 8-week pre-release campaign, release-day push,
content calendar per platform (TikTok/Instagram/YouTube/Spotify/SoundCloud),
pitch templates for [regional] radio stations and playlists,
international licensing strategy for diaspora markets,
and sync licensing opportunities."
```

### Film & Television
```
Prompt: "Develop a funding strategy for a [feature film/short film/TV series/documentary]
produced in [country].
Project: [title and 2-line pitch].
Budget: [USD amount].
Creative team: [description].
Caribbean/Latin American story: [describe regional authenticity].
Target audiences: [local/regional/international streaming].
Funding sources to pursue: [national film fund/co-production/streaming platform/
festivals/Caribbean Film Commission programs].
Create: investor pitch summary, co-production partnership approach,
streaming platform pitch strategy (Netflix LatAm, Amazon Prime LatAm, HBO Max LatAm),
festival strategy, and production incentive research."
```

### Artisan & Craft Businesses
```
Prompt: "Create a digital sales strategy for an artisan/craft business in [country].
Products: [Jamaican wood carvings/Haitian art/Colombian mochilas/
Peruvian textiles/Guatemalan weaving/Mexican Talavera/etc.].
Current sales: [local markets/tourist shops only].
Target: [international online market].
Platforms: [Etsy/Instagram/own website].
Shipping constraints: [from [country], international shipping challenges].
Create: product photography brief (without professional equipment),
product description templates,
pricing strategy for international market,
Instagram shop setup guide,
story/heritage narrative for product marketing,
and customs/export guide for handcrafts from [country]."
```

### Social Media Content Creation
```
Prompt: "Create a month of social media content for a [creative business type]
in [country/Caribbean/LatAm].
Business: [description]. Platform: [Instagram/TikTok/Facebook].
Voice: [fun/professional/cultural/educational].
Key themes: [local culture/craft/music/food/tourism].
Language: [English/Spanish/Portuguese/French/Creole].
Create: 30 post captions, 10 reel/TikTok concept descriptions,
8 story poll ideas, 5 hashtag strategies for [country] reach,
and 2 collaboration post concepts with local influencers."
```

### Content Localization
```
Prompt: "Localize this [marketing content/educational content/entertainment content]
for the [Caribbean/LatAm] market:
Original content: [paste content].
Source language: [language].
Target: [country/market].
Cultural adaptation needed: [list specific cultural references to adapt].
Maintain: [brand voice/key messages/specific terms].
Avoid: [culturally inappropriate references].
Provide: localized version with explanation of key adaptations made
and confidence level for any uncertain cultural elements."
```
