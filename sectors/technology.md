Major tech hubs in our region:

- **Mexico City/Guadalajara, Mexico**: Silicon Valley of Mexico — Clip, Konfío, Bitso
- **Medellín/Bogotá, Colombia**: Rappi, Addi, Habi

- **Santiago, Chile**: Cornershop, Fintual
- **Kingston, Jamaica**: Growing BPO+Tech hybrid
- **Bridgetown, Barbados**: Barbados Digital Village, offshore tech

## 40+ Technology AI Use Cases

### Software Development
```
Prompt: "I am a developer in [country] building [app type].
Tech stack: [list languages/frameworks].
Current challenge: [describe specific coding problem].
Code snippet: [paste code if applicable].
Performance constraint: [consider mobile-first for Latin America, low-bandwidth users].

Provide: solution with code example, explanation of approach,
alternative approaches and tradeoffs,
performance considerations for [country/region]'s internet infrastructure,
security considerations, and testing recommendations."
```

### Startup Strategy
```
Prompt: "Develop a go-to-market strategy for a tech startup in [country]
in the [industry] sector.
Product: [description]. Stage: [pre-product/MVP/post-revenue].
Target customers: [description]. Team: [skills].
Competitive landscape: [main competitors in [country/LATAM]].
Initial budget: [amount USD].

Strategy should include: market entry approach,
customer acquisition channels for [country] market,
pricing strategy (consider [country]'s economic context),
partnership opportunities (telcos, banks, distributors),
regional expansion roadmap (Phase 1: [country], Phase 2: [neighbors]),
and funding landscape ([country] angel investors, VC funds, accelerators)."
```

### AI Product Development
```
Prompt: "Design an AI-powered product for the [Caribbean/Latin American] market:
Problem to solve: [specific problem in the region].
Target users: [demographics, digital literacy level, language].
Technology: [what AI capabilities to leverage: NLP/vision/prediction].
Infrastructure reality: [mobile-first, variable internet, Spanish/Portuguese/French/Creole needed].
Monetization: [B2B/B2C/freemium model].

Design: product concept, key features,
AI model requirements,
multilingual considerations for the region,
data privacy compliance (LGPD Brazil/Data Protection Act Jamaica/GDPR for French territories),
MVP scope, and 6-month development roadmap."
```

### Digital Transformation for SMEs
```
Prompt: "Create a digital transformation roadmap for a [business type] SME in [country].
Current digital maturity: [basic/intermediate - describe what they currently use].
Business size: [X employees, revenue range].
Budget for technology: [amount in local currency].
Key pain points: [list 3-5 main operational problems].
Internet/connectivity: [quality in their location].

Recommend: prioritized digital tools (free or low-cost first),
AI tools they can implement this week (free tier),
12-month transformation roadmap,
ROI estimates per technology investment,
training plan for non-technical staff,
and local tech support resources in [country]."
```

### BPO/IT Services (Jamaica, Costa Rica, Colombia)
```
Prompt: "Develop an AI integration strategy for a [X]-seat BPO operation in [country].
Service lines: [customer service/data entry/technical support/etc.].
Current tools: [CRM/ticketing system].
Quality issues: [QA scores, CSAT, AHT].
Client requirements: [describe SLA requirements].
Staff profile: [education level, technical skills].

Recommend: AI tools for quality monitoring (automated call scoring),
agent assist tools to reduce AHT,
knowledge base AI for faster resolution,
workforce management AI,
implementation timeline,
expected impact on KPIs, and cost-benefit analysis."
```
