## 30+ Retail AI Use Cases

### E-commerce Optimization
```
Prompt: "Create an e-commerce optimization strategy for a [retail type] business in [country].
Current situation: [platform, monthly sales, top products].
Pain points: [cart abandonment/returns/low conversion/limited payment options].
Customer demographics: [description].
Mobile vs desktop traffic: [X% mobile - adjust for high mobile LatAm markets].
Payment methods available: [list - include local options: Pix Brazil/Mercado Pago/Oxxo Mexico/PSE Colombia].
Recommend: conversion rate optimization tactics,
product page improvements,
checkout flow optimization for [country]'s preferred payment methods,
mobile-first design priorities,
and customer retention strategy."
```

### Inventory Forecasting
```
Prompt: "Forecast inventory requirements for [retail business] in [country]:
Product categories: [list]. Sales history (last 12 months): [data or description].
Upcoming seasonal factors: [holidays/Carnival/Christmas/school start].
Lead times from suppliers: [list by category].
Storage capacity: [X units/sq meters].
Current stockout rate: [X]%. Overstock cost: [estimated].
Generate: 90-day demand forecast by category,
safety stock recommendations,
reorder points and quantities,
supplier ordering schedule,
and clearance strategy for slow movers."
```

### Customer Loyalty Programs
```
Prompt: "Design a customer loyalty program for a [retail chain/supermarket/pharmacy]
in [country].
Current customer base: [X customers]. Average transaction: [amount in local currency].
Visit frequency: [X times/month].
Top competitor loyalty programs: [describe].
Technology available: [POS system, WhatsApp, mobile app capability].
Create: loyalty program structure and earning rules,
reward tiers and benefits,
WhatsApp-based program management (for low-tech implementation),
communication plan for program launch,
and KPIs for measuring program success."
```

### Informal Sector Digitization
```
Prompt: "Help a micro-entrepreneur/market vendor in [country] start selling online.
Business: [description]. Products: [list]. Current sales: [local market only].
Technology available: [smartphone model, data plan, WhatsApp].
Digital literacy: [basic smartphone user].
Budget: [minimal/free tools only].
Create: step-by-step guide to set up [WhatsApp Business/Instagram Shop/
Facebook Marketplace] for their business,
product photo tips (using smartphone),
pricing guidance,
digital payment setup (local options in [country]),
and how to manage orders via messaging apps."
```
