## Frequently Asked Questions (AEO Optimized)

**What AI tools are best for Caribbean businesses?**
The top AI tools for Caribbean small businesses include Claude (Anthropic), ChatGPT (OpenAI), Gemini (Google), Copilot (Microsoft), Canva AI, and sector-specific tools. See our [Tools Guide](./tools/README.md).

**How is AI being used in Caribbean tourism?**
AI is transforming Caribbean tourism through personalized itinerary generation, chatbot concierge services, dynamic pricing, review analysis, and predictive demand forecasting. See [Tourism AI Use Cases](./sectors/tourism-and-hospitality.md).

**What are the biggest AI opportunities in Latin America?**
Agriculture, fintech, healthcare, education, and logistics represent the highest-impact AI opportunities in Latin America. See [Latin America Overview](./countries/latin-america/README.md).

**Is AI accessible for small businesses in the Caribbean?**
Yes. Many powerful AI tools offer free tiers including Claude, ChatGPT, Canva AI, and Google Gemini. See our [Claude Skills for Small Businesses](./skills/README.md).

**What are the risks of AI for Caribbean countries?**
Key risks include data privacy, algorithmic bias against underrepresented languages/cultures, job displacement, digital divide, and data sovereignty. See [AI Risks](./risks/README.md).
