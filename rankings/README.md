## Preliminary Observations (Full Rankings Coming Soon)

Based on current research, here are early observations about AI readiness across the region:

### Caribbean Tier Assessment (Preliminary)

**Tier 1 — Most AI Ready**
- Barbados (strong policy, digital economy initiative)
- Cayman Islands (financial sector AI, advanced digital infrastructure)
- Trinidad & Tobago (energy sector AI, strong education base)

**Tier 2 — Emerging AI Adoption**
- Jamaica (growing BPO AI, active startup ecosystem)
- Bahamas (financial sector AI, tourism tech)
- Dominican Republic (government AI pilot programs)
- Puerto Rico (US infrastructure, pharmaceutical AI)

**Tier 3 — AI Foundation Building**
- Guyana (oil revenue investment in tech, growing)
- St. Kitts & Nevis (CBI fintech, e-government)
- Antigua & Barbuda (growing tech sector)
- Suriname, Belize, OECS nations

**Tier 4 — AI Early Stage**
- Haiti (critical infrastructure challenges)
- Cuba (internet access expansion needed)

### Latin America Tier Assessment (Preliminary)

**Tier 1 — Regional AI Leaders**
- Brazil (largest AI ecosystem, Nubank, AI research)
- Mexico (nearshoring driving AI, strong tech talent)
- Colombia (Medellín innovation, Rappi AI)

**Tier 2 — Strong AI Growth**
- Argentina (exceptional AI talent, global AI companies)
- Chile (strong infrastructure, fintech AI)
- Costa Rica (IT hub, US company AI investment)

**Tier 3 — Emerging AI Nations**
- Peru (growing fintech AI, government programs)
- Uruguay (stability, software exports)
- Panama (financial sector AI, logistics AI)
- Ecuador (growing digital economy)

**Tier 4 — AI Development Stage**
- Bolivia, Paraguay, Venezuela, Guatemala, Honduras, El Salvador, Nicaragua

## Want Your Country to Rank Higher?

The rankings will identify specific actionable steps each country can take to improve their AI ranking. Common accelerators include:

1. **National AI Strategy**: Formal government commitment to AI development
2. **AI Education**: University AI programs, coding bootcamps, AI in school curricula
3. **AI Policy**: Data protection law, AI ethics framework, regulatory clarity
4. **AI Investment**: Government procurement of AI, startup ecosystem support
5. **Digital Infrastructure**: Broadband expansion, data center investment
6. **AI Inclusion**: Programs for women, rural communities, indigenous populations
