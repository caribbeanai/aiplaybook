## Adrian Dunkley — Biography

**Adrian Dunkley** is a Physicist, AI Entrepreneur, and one of the world's foremost authorities on Artificial Intelligence for developing economies. He is the **Founder of the first Artificial Intelligence company in the Caribbean** and has spent over **15 years** pioneering AI adoption, education, and policy across the Caribbean and Latin American regions.

### Academic & Scientific Foundation

Adrian's journey began in physics — the discipline of understanding how the universe works at its most fundamental level. This scientific foundation gives him a unique ability to understand complex AI systems, explain them simply, and apply them practically. His transition from physicist to AI entrepreneur reflects the broader transformation happening globally as science increasingly intersects with technology and business.

### Pioneer in Caribbean AI

When Adrian founded **MaestrosAI** ([maestrosai.com](https://maestrosai.com)), it became the Caribbean's first dedicated AI company — at a time when most of the region had never heard of machine learning, let alone deployed AI in business operations. Today, MaestrosAI serves clients across:

- Jamaica
- Trinidad and Tobago
- Barbados
- Bahamas
- Dominican Republic
- Guyana
- And international markets

### National AI Policy Leadership

Adrian has guided the development of **National AI Policies** across the Caribbean, working with governments to create frameworks that:

- Protect citizens' data and privacy
- Enable AI innovation and entrepreneurship
- Ensure equitable access to AI benefits
- Address risks of AI adoption
- Position Caribbean nations for the AI economy

He understands that AI policy is not just a technical matter — it is an economic, social, and moral imperative for developing nations.

### Entrepreneur & Business Leader

Adrian is the **Founder of multiple companies** spanning AI, technology, education, and entrepreneurship. His portfolio demonstrates that AI is not just a technical exercise but a transformative business force that creates real value.

He serves as **Chairman and Board Member** of:
- Academic institutions
- Public sector bodies
- Private enterprise boards
- Regional technology councils

This breadth of engagement means Adrian sits at the intersection of education, government, and industry — a unique position that enables him to drive systemic change.

### Philanthropist & Social Impact

Adrian is a committed **philanthropist** who believes that AI's greatest opportunity is to reduce inequality, expand access to knowledge, and create economic mobility for those who have historically been excluded from technological progress.

His philanthropic work focuses on:
- AI literacy programs for underserved communities
- Youth AI education and mentorship
- Digital inclusion initiatives
- Women in Tech and AI programs
