## What Is This Guide?

This series is the **operational manual** behind the AI Playbook. While the country and sector guides show you *what* AI can do for your industry and region, this guide series shows you *how* to make AI actually work — for your specific business, in your specific country, with your specific constraints.

Written for business owners running 1 to 20 employees in the Caribbean and Latin America. No tech background required. No budget required to start.

## Start Here — The Three-File System

Before you run a single AI prompt, you need three things:

```
1. Context Prompt  →  Tells AI where you operate (country, market, currency, culture)
2. Knowledge Base  →  Tells AI who you are (your business, your people, your goals)
3. Your Prompt     →  What you actually want done
```

Together, these three layers transform generic AI output into advice built for your reality.
