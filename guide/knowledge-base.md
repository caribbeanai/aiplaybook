## What Is a Knowledge Base?

Your Knowledge Base is a document that captures everything an AI needs to know about *your specific business* before you ask it anything.

Two businesses in the same city, same industry, same size can get completely different — and completely right — AI advice if they each have a good Knowledge Base. Without it, the AI gives you generic answers. With it, the AI gives you answers built for your situation.

**You write it once. You paste it (or attach it) alongside your [Context Prompt](./context-prompt.md) at the start of every AI conversation. You update it when things change.**

## English Instructions {#english-instructions}

### How to Create Your Knowledge Base

1. Create a new document (Word, Google Docs, Notes app on your phone — anything works)
2. Name it: **My Business Knowledge Base — [Your Business Name]**
3. Work through each section below, answering the questions in plain language
4. Write the way you would talk to a smart advisor over coffee — honest, direct, specific
5. Save the document somewhere you can easily copy from
6. **How to use it**: Paste the full text (or attach the file) in your AI conversation, immediately after your [Context Prompt](./context-prompt.md), before asking your question

### The Knowledge Base Template (English)

Copy the template below. Fill in every section. The more specific you are, the better your AI outputs will be.

---

```
KNOWLEDGE BASE — [YOUR BUSINESS NAME]
Last Updated: [Month, Year]

─────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 1: THE BASICS
─────────────────────────────────────
Business name: [Your business name]

What I sell or provide: [Describe your product or service in 2–3 sentences]

Location: [Country / City / Neighbourhood — include if neighbourhood matters for
your business, e.g. "New Kingston, Jamaica" vs "rural St. Elizabeth, Jamaica"]

How long I have been operating: [e.g. 6 months / 3 years / 12 years]

Legal status: [e.g. sole trader / partnership / limited liability company (LLC) /
registered with [local business registration body]]

─────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 2: MY PEOPLE
─────────────────────────────────────
Total people working in the business (including me): [number]

Who does what:
- Me (owner): [your main roles — e.g. I do everything: sales, delivery, accounts]
- [Name or Role 1]: [what they do]
- [Name or Role 2]: [what they do]
- [Any contractors or part-time staff]: [describe]

Skills we have in-house: [e.g. carpentry, bookkeeping, customer service, social media]

Skills we are missing: [e.g. graphic design, web development, financial analysis,
legal knowledge, marketing strategy]

─────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 3: MY PRODUCT OR SERVICE
─────────────────────────────────────
What exactly I offer:
[List your main products or services. Be specific.]
- [Product/Service 1]: [brief description, price range]
- [Product/Service 2]: [brief description, price range]
- [Product/Service 3]: [brief description, price range]

How customers buy from me: [e.g. walk-in / WhatsApp orders / Instagram DMs /
phone calls / website / referral only]

What sells well: [your top 2–3 sellers]

What does not sell / moves slowly: [honest answer]

How I make the product or deliver the service: [Do you make it yourself? Source
locally? Import? Subcontract? How does the delivery or fulfilment work?]

─────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 4: MY CUSTOMERS
─────────────────────────────────────
Who buys from me (be specific, not generic):
[e.g. "Mainly women aged 30–55 from the middle-income neighbourhoods in [city]
who want handmade gifts for occasions. They find me through Instagram and
WhatsApp word of mouth."]

Where they find me: [e.g. Instagram / word of mouth / foot traffic / Google /
referrals from past clients]

Why they choose me over competitors: [your honest answer — what do they say?]

What they complain about: [be honest — this helps the AI solve real problems]

How they pay: [e.g. cash / bank transfer / card machine / Linx / PayNow /
PIX / Nequi / mobile money / mix of the above]

Seasonal patterns in my customers: [e.g. "December is my busiest month —
about 40% of my annual revenue. August is slowest."]

─────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 5: MY MONEY
─────────────────────────────────────
Monthly revenue (approximate range): [e.g. $50,000–$80,000 JMD / $5,000–$8,000 BBD]
Note: You do not need exact figures. Ballpark is fine.

My biggest expenses each month:
- [Expense 1]: [amount or percentage of revenue]
- [Expense 2]: [amount or percentage]
- [Expense 3]: [amount or percentage]

Am I profitable, breaking even, or still investing?
[Honest answer: e.g. "Profitable but tightly — I pay myself about X per month."
or "Breaking even right now — I expect profitability in 6 months."]

How I am funded: [e.g. entirely from revenue / family loan / bank loan /
government grant / savings / mix]

What financial tools I use: [e.g. Excel spreadsheet / a notebook / QuickBooks /
Wave Accounting / my accountant handles it / nothing formal yet]

─────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 6: WHAT MAKES ME DIFFERENT
─────────────────────────────────────
My competitive edge (the thing my regulars say about me):
[e.g. "Fastest turnaround in town." / "Best price for quality." /
"Only person in the area who does [specific service]." /
"Clients trust me because I've been here 10 years and I know everyone."]

What I do that competitors do not:
[be specific — even small things count]

─────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 7: WHERE I AM BEHIND
─────────────────────────────────────
Where competitors are ahead of me:
[e.g. "My main competitor has a website and takes online bookings. I don't." /
"They have two delivery vehicles. I rely on renting." /
"Their social media is much more active than mine."]

What I am not good at (honest):
[e.g. "I am not consistent with social media." /
"My bookkeeping is a mess." / "I struggle with collections from slow-paying clients." /
"I have no system for follow-up after a sale."]

─────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 8: MY GOALS
─────────────────────────────────────
Where I want to be in 6 months:
[Be specific: e.g. "Add two new corporate catering clients by March." /
"Open a second location." / "Launch an online store." /
"Reduce my own working hours from 70 to 50 per week."]

Where I want to be in 1 year:
[Be specific]

Where I want to be in 3 years:
[e.g. grow / stabilise / export / franchise / bring in a partner /
make enough to stop working weekends / hire a manager]

─────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 9: MY CURRENT STRATEGY
─────────────────────────────────────
What I am doing right now to reach those goals:
[Describe your current approach to growth, marketing, operations improvement]

What I have tried that worked:
[Even one or two things — what moved the needle?]

What I have tried that did not work:
[Be honest — this saves the AI from suggesting the same failed approaches]

What I am doing right now to bring in customers:
[e.g. "Mostly word of mouth and Instagram. I post inconsistently.
I've tried Facebook ads once — spent $50 USD and got nothing."]

─────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 10: MY CONSTRAINTS
─────────────────────────────────────
What is holding me back (be blunt):
[e.g. "Cash flow is my biggest issue — I sometimes can't restock because clients
pay 60 days late." / "I have no one to delegate to, so everything waits for me." /
"My internet is unreliable and I can't afford reliable hosting for a website." /
"I don't know how to do accounting and can't afford to hire someone yet." /
"Import lead times mean I run out of stock at peak season."]

My budget for new tools or investments right now:
[e.g. "Nothing extra right now." / "About $50 USD/month." / "Up to $200 USD for a
one-off setup."]

Time I can realistically spend on new initiatives per week:
[e.g. "Maybe 2 hours, on evenings." / "I have Saturdays free." /
"Right now, nothing extra — I am at capacity."]

─────────────────────────────────────
END OF KNOWLEDGE BASE
─────────────────────────────────────
```

### Keeping Your Knowledge Base Alive

Update it when:
- You hire or lose a staff member
- You add or drop a product/service
- Your revenue changes significantly
- You reach or change a goal
- Something big happens in your market or industry
- Your biggest constraint changes

**Five minutes of updates every month keeps every AI conversation grounded in where your business actually is — not where it was.**

## Enstriksyon an Kreyòl {#enstriksyon-an-kreyòl}

### Kijan Pou Kreye Baz Konesans ou

1. Kreye yon nouvo dokiman (Word, Google Docs, nòt sou telefòn — nenpòt bagay mache)
2. Ba li non: **Baz Konesans Mwen — [Non Biznis ou]**
3. Travay chak seksyon ki anba a, reponn kesyon yo nan lang senp
4. Ekri jan ou ta pale ak yon konsitan entèlijan nan yon kafe — onèt, dirèk, espesifik
5. Sove dokiman an yon kote ou ka fasil kopye li
6. **Kijan pou itilize li**: Kole tèks konplè a (oswa ajoute fichye a) nan konvèsasyon IA ou, imedyatman aprè [Prompt Kontèks ou](./context-prompt.md), anvan ou poze kesyon ou

```
BAZ KONESANS — [NON BIZNIS OU]
```
